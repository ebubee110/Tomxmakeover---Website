Our Beauty Services

makeup

Every face tells a story, and makeup helps you tell it with
confidence. Whether you're walking down the aisle, stepping
out for a special event, or simply want to look and feel your
best, our makeup sessions are tailored to enhance your
natural beauty. We don’t just apply products—we create looks
that turn heads and leave lasting impressions. Let your face be
the canvas, and let’s paint something beautiful together.

gele styling

A gele isn’t just a headwrap—it’s tradition, identity, and
elegance woven into one. We understand the art and meaning
behind every pleat and fold. Whether you prefer bold and
structured or soft and graceful, our gele styles complete your
look with a touch of cultural pride. Step into the room with
poise, power, and heritage wrapped perfectly on your crown.

lash extension

Sometimes, all it takes is the perfect set of lashes to elevate
your entire look. Our lash extensions are designed to frame
your eyes, highlight your features, and give you that effortless
wow effect—day or night. No need for mascara, no stress. Just
full, fluffy lashes that last and leave you feeling gorgeous.

microblading

Imagine waking up with perfectly shaped brows—no pencil, no
gel, just effortless beauty. Microblading is our go-to solution
for sparse, uneven, or undefined brows. With delicate, hair-
like strokes, we craft brows that match your facial structure
and reflect your style. It’s not just a trend—it’s a beauty
investment that keeps giving.

microshading

If you're after soft, natural-looking brows with a powdery
finish, microshading is the answer. Ideal for all skin types,
it blends precision with subtlety to give you that flawless
brow definition without looking too bold. Our technique ensures
your brows stay filled and fabulous, saving you time
and keeping you ready for any moment.
